# Employee management

> **Stub.** Documents the employee admin screen.

## Adding employees

The preferred way to add an employee is to **invite** them by email — see [Invite your team](../getting-started/invite-team). The invitation flow handles account creation, links existing accounts when relevant, and assigns the role.

Administrators can also create an employee record directly from **Admin → Employees → Add**, but this skips the invitation email. Use it for placeholder records or imported data.

## Editing employees

From **Admin → Employees**:

- Update name, email, phone
- Change role
- Change office assignment
- Set or update pay rate
- Reset password (sends a reset link)
- Activate / deactivate

## Bulk operations

You can deactivate multiple employees at once — useful at the end of tax season. Select the employees and choose **Bulk Action → Deactivate**.

## Deactivating vs. deleting

Deactivating an employee is the standard way to remove someone. Deactivated employees can't sign in but their history stays. There's no "delete employee" — history is preserved for legal/audit reasons.
