# Common issues

> **Stub.** Living list of common questions and how to resolve them.

## I can't sign in

- **Forgot password?** Click **Forgot password** on the login screen. The reset email arrives within a couple of minutes — check spam if you don't see it.
- **Account deactivated?** If you've left a firm, your account at that firm may have been deactivated. If you have linked accounts at other firms, you can still sign in there.
- **Wrong tenant?** If you have linked accounts at multiple firms, you'll be asked which one to sign into after entering your credentials.

## I clocked in at the wrong time

Ask your supervisor or manager to correct it. They can edit any time clock entry from the **Time Clock → History** view. All corrections are audited so there's no concern about the change being hidden.

## My report is missing an employee

Check:

1. The employee is **active** (not deactivated).
2. The employee is assigned to the office you're filtering by, if you're filtering by office.
3. The employee actually has time clock entries in the date range.

## CSV export looks weird in Excel

WebCenter's CSVs are UTF-8 with a byte-order mark, which Excel handles correctly. If you're seeing corrupted characters, open the CSV in Excel via **Data → From Text** and explicitly select **UTF-8** as the encoding.

## I'm an employee at multiple firms — which one do I see?

After signing in, you'll see a tenant picker if your account spans multiple firms. The currently active firm is shown in the top navigation. Click it to switch.

Each firm only sees their own data — the other firms you work for are never visible to your employer.

## Something else?

Email support from inside the app (**Help → Contact Support**) and include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- The approximate time it happened (so we can find it in the logs)
- Browser and device, if relevant

Pedant Studios is a small team, but support requests are typically answered within one business day.
