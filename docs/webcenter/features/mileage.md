# Mileage tracking

> **Stub.** Documents the mileage-tracking feature for firms whose employees drive between client sites.

## When to use it

Mileage tracking is for employees who drive as part of their work — between client sites, between offices, or for errands. It's optional. If your firm doesn't reimburse mileage, you can leave it disabled.

## Logging mileage

1. From the time clock screen, click **Add Mileage**.
2. Enter the start odometer reading, end odometer reading, and a description.
3. Save.

Mileage entries are tied to the employee and date and show up in the Mileage Report.

## Rates

Mileage reimbursement rates are configured per tenant under **Settings → Mileage**. The default is the current IRS standard rate, but you can override.

## Reports

The Mileage Report exports to CSV, formatted to drop straight into your payroll provider's expense-reimbursement workflow.
