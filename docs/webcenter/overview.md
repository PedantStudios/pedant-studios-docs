# WebCenter

WebCenter is a time clock and workforce management application built for tax preparation and bookkeeping firms. It handles time tracking, scheduling, mileage, employee management, messaging, and reporting — everything a small firm needs to run a workforce that scales up during tax season and down again afterward.

## Who it's for

- Independent tax preparation offices
- Tax-prep franchise locations (Liberty Tax, Jackson Hewitt, etc.)
- Enrolled Agent practices
- Tax resolution firms
- Small CPA firms doing tax (1–5 CPAs + support staff)
- Bookkeeping firms

If your team grows 3× during tax season and shrinks afterward, WebCenter is built for that pattern.
