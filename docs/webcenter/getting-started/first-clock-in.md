# Your first clock-in

> **Stub.** Walks an employee through clocking in for the first time, including tablet vs. phone setups.

## Web (desktop or mobile browser)

1. Sign in at your firm's WebCenter URL.
2. Click **Clock In**.
3. Confirm any required fields (office, position, mileage start if your firm tracks it).
4. You're clocked in. The button now reads **Clock Out**.

## What happens behind the scenes

- WebCenter records the time you clocked in to the second.
- Depending on your firm's rounding settings, the displayed time may be rounded to the nearest 15 minutes (or whatever interval your firm has configured).
- The actual time is always preserved and available in reports — rounding is for display only.

## Clock-out

When you're done, click **Clock Out**. The same screen will let you log mileage if your firm uses that feature.

## Mistakes happen

If you forgot to clock in or clock out, your supervisor or manager can adjust your time after the fact. See [Time clock](../features/time-clock#corrections) for details.
