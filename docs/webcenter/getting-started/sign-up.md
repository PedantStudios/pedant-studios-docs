# Sign up for WebCenter

> **Stub.** This page will walk a new firm through creating their account and tenant.

## What you'll need

- An email address (you'll use this to sign in)
- Your firm's name
- The address of your primary office

## Steps

1. Visit the WebCenter sign-up page.
2. Enter your email and create a password.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Set up your firm — name, primary office, and time zone.
5. You're in. Next, [invite your team](./invite-team).

## Free vs. Paid

WebCenter is free for one location with unlimited employees. The Paid tier (~$29/location/month) unlocks multi-location, scheduling, time-off requests, audit logs, and priority support. You can upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**.

See the [pricing page](https://pedantstudios.com/webcenter/pricing) *(coming soon)* for details.
