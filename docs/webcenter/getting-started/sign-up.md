# Sign up for WebCenter

Signing up creates a new firm in WebCenter and makes you its first administrator. The whole flow takes a couple of minutes and uses an email confirmation step before your firm is fully activated.

## Before you start

Have ready:

- An email address you can check right now (you'll click a confirmation link)
- A name for your firm — this is what employees and reports will show
- A name for your first office (for many firms this is the firm name + the city, e.g., "Smith Tax — Sacramento")

## Steps

1. Visit the WebCenter sign-up page.
2. Fill in:
   - **Workplace name** — Your firm's name.
   - **Initial location name** — Your first office.
   - **Your name** — First and last.
   - **Username** — 3–32 characters; letters, numbers, dots, hyphens, and underscores. Choose something memorable; usernames don't change later.
   - **Password** — Minimum 12 characters. Use a passphrase if that's easier than a string of symbols.
   - **Email** — Where the confirmation link goes.
3. Submit the form.
4. WebCenter sends a confirmation email and shows you a "check your email" message. Don't refresh, close, or come back to this — proceed to step 5.
5. Continue at **[Confirm your email](./email-confirmation)** to finish setup.

Your firm isn't actually created until you click the link in the email. That's intentional: it prevents typos and abandoned signups from leaving phantom firms in the system.

## What gets created when you confirm

When you complete the email confirmation, WebCenter sets up:

- Your firm (the **tenant**) at its starting plan: **Free**, with a 14-day trial available.
- Your initial office.
- Your administrator account, with the username and password you provided.
- Your timezone — set during confirmation; defaults to the timezone your browser reports.

## Free tier limits at sign-up

The Free plan starts you with:

- **Unlimited employees** — invite as many as you need.
- **1 location** — add more by starting a trial or upgrading.
- **1 admin** — typically the founder; additional admins require a paid plan.
- **90 days of history** — older time clock and mileage data is hidden from reports until you upgrade.

You can start the trial or upgrade any time from **Settings → Billing**.

## Already have an account at another firm?

If you've signed up to WebCenter before — at a different firm — and used the same email, the confirmation step lets you **link** your existing account. That way you only have one set of credentials for all the firms you work with on WebCenter.

See **[Working at multiple firms](./multi-employer)** for how multi-firm accounts behave.
